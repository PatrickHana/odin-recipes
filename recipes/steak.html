Steak

A Perfectly Cooked Medium-Rare Steak

Enjoy this steak with a glass of red wine!

Ingredients

  • Rib Eye Steak
  • Butter
  • Rose Mary
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the stea with salt & pepper
  2. Sear the steak in a cast iron pan with butter.
  3. Add rose mary to the butter and baste until cooked to medium-rare
  4. Let rest for 30 minutes then slice into strips and enjoy!
